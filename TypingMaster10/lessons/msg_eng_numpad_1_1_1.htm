The 10-key number pad, also called numeric keypad, is a very effective and fast tool for keying in series of numbers.

In this three lesson course, you will learn to use the number pad efficiently with touch typing technique.

Data entry speed is often measured in Keystrokes per Hour (KPH). To convert Words per Minute (WPM) to KPH simply multiply the WPM result by 300.

Alternatively, if you have set the speed unit to Keystrokes per Minute (KPM), you can multiply the KPM result by 60.
