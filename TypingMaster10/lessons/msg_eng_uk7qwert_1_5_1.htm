Typing games are a fun way to warm up your fingers before lessons or take a short break from your studies. The Bubbles game is the easiest for a beginning typist.

If you like playing the games, you could take a game break half way through each lesson for a little variety.

To Play the Games
1. Click "OK"
2. Click on "Games" in the menu
3. To return to the typing lesson, click on "Courses" in the program menu
