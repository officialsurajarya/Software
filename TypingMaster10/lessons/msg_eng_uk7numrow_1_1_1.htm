In the next two lessons you will learn to type numbers on the number row. Before you start, take a quick look at which finger to use.

Left hand

  • Index finger (F) reaches 4 and 5
  • Middle finger (D) reaches 3
  • Ring finger (S) reaches 2
  • Little finger (A) reaches 1

Right hand

  • Index finger (J) reaches 6 and 7
  • Middle finger (K) reaches 8
  • Ring finger (L) reaches 9
  • Little finger (;) reaches 0

Note about split keyboards
On split keyboards the key for number 6 is often on the left side. In this case, use the left index finger to type 6.
