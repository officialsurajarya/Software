Place your right hand on the number pad like this:

  • Index finger on 4
  • Middle finger on 5
  • Ring finger on 6
  • Thumb on 0
  • Little finger on Enter

From these home keys:

  • Index finger reaches numbers 7 and 1
  • Middle finger reaches numbers 8 and 2, and the division (/) key
  • Ring finger reaches numbers 9 and 3, the multiplication (*) key and the decimal point. We also recommend to use it for the subtraction (-) key as this is easier than using your little finger.
  • Little finger reaches the addition (+) key
